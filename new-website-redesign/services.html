Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

What conditions can chiropractic care help with? +
Chiropractic care is highly effective for back pain, neck pain, headaches, migraines, sciatica, sports injuries, auto accident injuries, joint pain, and many musculoskeletal conditions. We focus on treating the root cause, not just masking symptoms.
Is chiropractic care safe, and are the adjustments painful? +
Yes, chiropractic care is extremely safe when performed by a licensed professional. Most adjustments are not painful—many patients describe immediate relief and improved mobility. We tailor every adjustment to your comfort level and health needs.
How many sessions will I need to see results? +
Every patient is different. Some experience relief after 1-2 sessions, while others with chronic conditions may need ongoing care. During your evaluation, we'll give you a clear, honest timeline based on your specific condition—no guesswork or endless appointments.
Do you accept insurance? +
We operate outside the insurance system to provide you with transparent pricing and avoid the bureaucratic delays. This allows us to focus 100% on your care, not dealing with insurance companies. We offer affordable cash rates and flexible payment options.
What should I expect during my first visit? +
Your first visit includes a comprehensive 30-minute evaluation where we'll review your health history, discuss your symptoms and goals, perform a physical assessment, and create a personalized treatment plan. If appropriate, we may begin treatment during your first visit.
What is dry needling and how is it different from acupuncture? +
Dry needling is a western medicine technique that targets trigger points and muscle dysfunction using thin needles. Unlike acupuncture (which is based on traditional Chinese medicine), dry needling is based on anatomy and neuromuscular science to release muscle tension and improve mobility.
Can I combine different treatments in one visit? +
Absolutely! We often combine multiple therapies for optimal results. For example, a typical session might include spinal manipulation, soft-tissue therapy, and therapeutic exercises. Dr. Allen will create a personalized treatment plan based on your specific needs.
