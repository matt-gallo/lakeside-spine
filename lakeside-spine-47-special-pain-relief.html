Is this visit good for back pain or sciatica?

Absolutely. Back pain, sciatica, and hip/knee issues are some of the most common reasons active adults come in.

Can this help with running injuries?

Yes. We run orthopedic tests specifically for runners: stride issues, hip stability, ankle mechanics, and more.

What if I'm not currently in pain?

Many active adults use this visit to prevent injuries and fix movement issues before they become problems.

Do you accept insurance?

We're a 100% cash-based clinic. That keeps pricing predictable and lets Dr. Allen deliver the care you need without insurance restrictions. If you'd like to submit for out-of-network reimbursement, we'll provide an itemized receipt you can use with your carrier.

What should I wear to the appointment?

Wear clothing you can easily move in—think athletic wear, jeans with stretch, or work attire you're comfortable stretching in.

How long will the first appointment take?

Plan for 45-60 minutes. We cover your consultation, movement testing, adjustment, and education in that first session.

Is this safe if I've had a previous injury or surgery?

Absolutely. Dr. Allen tailors every adjustment based on your history and collaborates with your physicians as needed.
