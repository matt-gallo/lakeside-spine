General Care

Chiropractic care is a healthcare discipline that focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal disorders, particularly those affecting the spine. Through manual adjustments and manipulations, chiropractors restore proper alignment, reduce pain, improve mobility, and support your body's natural healing process.

Yes, chiropractic care is extremely safe when performed by a licensed professional like Dr. Andrew Allen. Serious complications are very rare. Most patients experience immediate relief and improved mobility with minimal to no discomfort during treatment.

Your first visit includes a comprehensive 30-minute evaluation where we'll review your health history, discuss your symptoms and goals, perform a physical assessment, and create a personalized treatment plan. If appropriate, we may begin treatment during your first visit. You'll leave with a clear understanding of your condition and the path forward.

Almost anyone! Whether you're dealing with chronic pain, recovering from an injury, looking to improve athletic performance, or simply want to feel better and move more freely—chiropractic care can help. We treat patients of all ages and activity levels.

Treatments

We use a variety of evidence-based techniques including spinal manipulation, soft-tissue therapy, dry needling, cupping therapy, posture analysis, and therapeutic exercises. Treatment is customized based on your specific condition and comfort level.

Most patients experience no pain during adjustments. You may hear a "popping" sound, which is simply gas being released from the joint—it's not bones cracking. Some patients feel immediate relief, while others may experience mild soreness similar to post-workout muscle fatigue, which typically resolves within 24 hours.

Every patient is different. Some experience relief after 1-2 sessions, while others with chronic conditions may need ongoing care. During your initial evaluation, we'll give you a clear, honest timeline based on your specific condition—no guesswork or endless appointments. Our goal is to get you better, not to keep you coming back forever.

Dry needling is a technique that uses thin needles to release muscle tension and trigger points. It's highly effective for treating chronic pain, muscle tightness, and sports injuries. Unlike acupuncture, dry needling is based on Western medicine principles and targets specific muscular trigger points.

Auto Accidents

Absolutely. We specialize in treating auto accident injuries including whiplash, neck pain, back pain, and soft tissue damage. Early treatment is crucial for full recovery. We provide comprehensive care and can document your injuries for insurance claims if needed.

As soon as possible. Many auto accident injuries don't show symptoms immediately due to adrenaline and shock. Early evaluation and treatment can prevent chronic pain and long-term complications. We recommend being seen within the first 72 hours after an accident.

Most auto insurance policies include medical payment coverage (MedPay) or personal injury protection (PIP) that covers chiropractic treatment. We can provide documentation and reports for your insurance claim, though you are responsible for payment at the time of service.

Whiplash is a neck injury caused by rapid back-and-forth movement, common in rear-end collisions. Symptoms include neck pain, stiffness, headaches, and dizziness. We treat whiplash with gentle spinal adjustments, soft-tissue therapy, and rehabilitative exercises to restore proper movement and reduce inflammation.
